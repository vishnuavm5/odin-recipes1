Chilli Parota

Description

Ingrediants

  • 3 parotta
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • ½ tsp fennel / saunf
  • few curry leaves
  • ½ onion (cubed)
  • 1 chilli (slit)
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • ½ capsicum (cubed)
  • ¼ tsp kashmiri chilli powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • ½ tsp coriander powder
  • 2 tbsp tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 2 tbsp coriander (finely chopp
  1. Firstly, cut 3 parotta into small pieces. you can use fresh or leftover parotta. heat 2 tbsp oil and splutter ½ tsp fennel and few curry leaves. add ½ onion, 1 chilli and 1 tsp ginger garlic paste.
  2. saute well until onions shrink slightly. further, add ½ capsicum and stir fry. also, add ¼ tsp chilli powder, ½ tsp garam masala, ½ tsp coriander powder.
